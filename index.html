THE STORY OF GUYASA

Kichwa Hunters from the Amazon rainforest are known for their long lasting hunts, and sharp senses. But this has not always been like this! In ancient times die Kichwa tribe people were lazy, slowly, unorganzied and sometimes they even got lost in the jungle. This is what happens to “GUYA” the Kichwa who first tasted the power of Guayusa, a leaf so powerful that even the laziest Kichwa will turn into an unstoppable human being. “GUYA” stands for the cup Kichwa people drink Guayusa from.

This was the start of the arising Guayusa leaf through the Kichwa tribes around the whole Amazon rainforest. till today the Kichwa people wake up in the middle of the night to boil guayusa leafs, drink them and go for hunting. The legend says that since then the Kichwa people became the most active and successful tribes in the Amazon as hunters and farmers. Nowadays every family ownes at least one Guayusa tree. They kept their secret for thousands of years not because they did not want to share, but because no one seemed to be in need of it.

This changed! The Kichwa people see the need for all humans to energize their mind, start thinking in a holistic way with long time horizon.

They believe, that the power of the Guayusa spirit can change the word for better!

GUYA tries to hunt for hours without any result. He can’t focus, his steps making to much noise and he feels heavy and lazy.

He even fails to hit the most laziest looking animal in the jungle.

GUYA feels depressed, unable to catch food for his tribe. Again he is trapped in the jungle, not even knowing where he is lazy.

The nights in the Amazon Rainforest are everything but not bright. GUYA decides to sleep under a tree and search for the way back home as soon as it gets morning.

He slept deep with very clear dreams in which the tree he sleeps under starts talking to him.

“GUYA, I see you for years roaming around like some bumbling idiot, never ever hitting any animal with your arrows, always loose orientation and then rest for hours without any success!

Take my powerful leafs. They are looking unremarkable but they will give you exactly what you and your people need! Just eat them or boil them in water and drink it.

My “Guayusa” leafs will give you strength, persistency, sharpen your senses, increase your focus and let you feel energized and happy over hours!

When GUYA waked up it was still night but he remembered every word the tree said. First he considered it as nonsense but then decided to give it a try.

He took some leafs from the tree ate them and gave thanks to trees spirit.

He started walking through the night forest but found it surprisingly bright.

He found some eatable mushrooms and fruits and started a fire to keep himself warm and eat.

Soon he recognized noises around him in the dark forest. Like a gentle walk.

He saw the glowing eyes of a puma hiding in between the shadows of the bushes around him.

In this moment he felt the rising power from inside. The sprits of the eaten Guayusa leafs arises.

He felt cam, relaxed, focused and cool-headed.

This enabled him to take a rapid action and grab is bow and arrow he put beside.

In not even a seconds targeted the puma. full of self confidence and strong aura

The puma left without even attacking. GUYA was surprised by his own actions.

With the starting morning sun he came back home.

Calling his people together and telling them about his experiences and the amazing leaf he found.

WHY SO SPECIAL?

Guayusa is Not a secret plant from the Amazon rainforest. Not noted from western world for hundreds of years this plant is used for thousands of years by the ingenious Kichwa tribe.

It is the most caffeinated leaf in the world but also combines active substances like Theobromine, Theophylline and L-Theanine that are normally not found together in this specific amount.

Guayusa therefore provides a new energy that is more long lasting, focusing and mostly activates your mind rather than your cardiovascular system. The extract of Guayusa leafs has around 20% - 22% caffeine and 30% Antioxidants, thats the riche concentrate in the world.

The plant grown in permacultures and protects this way the Amazon rainforest from deforestation.
